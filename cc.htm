Chris Curry

My name is Christopher Curry. Being born and raised in the Triple Cities has given me an opportunity to grow familiar with the cultures and diversity of our area. As entertainers, the staff of Ricky T's understands that we are a very pivotal part of your event. The memories you take away will be for a lifetime. 

 Entertainment is more than a good beat and some flashy lights. It's being personable, comfortable and building a relationship that will last long after the event is over.

Thank you to all of the people I have served so far and I hope to include you to this long list of what I call friends. I would enjoy to be your DJ when you use Ricky T's for your event.

Best wishes!
